About me

Have you ever looked at a painting and felt connected instantly? I did, I loved art from a young age. As a creative person, I always looked for ways to express my personality through art. Now, as a highly-skilled and creative artist, a graphic designer, and a leather accessories designer, I paint portraits and larger scale paintings using oil paint and other materials. I have extended experience in the restoration and reproduction of artifacts in religious temples and other artwork. While using specific skills and techniques through my restoration work, I am keeping up to date with trendy movements and innovations technology has to offer. What I hear from colleagues and clients-“Vasily’s work is moving, eye-catching and engaging” While doing restoration work in temples in Poland and Ukraine, working on portraits for private clients - I am excited about the future, I am open to business opportunities and welcome business offers.
